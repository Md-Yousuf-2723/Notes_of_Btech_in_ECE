## 📚 Some Ayahs & Hadiths on Sharing Knowledge

**Quran, Surah Ta-Ha (20:114)**
Allah taught the Prophet ﷺ to make this dua: "My Lord! Increase me in knowledge." Even the Prophet ﷺ was told to keep asking Allah for more knowledge — showing all knowledge comes from Him alone.

**Quran, Surah Az-Zumar (39:9)**
Allah asks: are those who know and those who don't know ever equal? This shows knowledge is a favor, not a personal achievement to boast about.

**Quran, Surah Al-Mujadila (58:11)**
Allah raises the believers who have knowledge in rank — but only when it is paired with faith and good action, not knowledge alone.

**Quran, Surah An-Nahl (16:125)**
Allah tells the Prophet ﷺ to invite people to the right path with wisdom and good teaching — showing that sharing knowledge gently is a command, not optional.

---

**Hadith — Sahih Muslim 1631**
The Prophet ﷺ said a person's good deeds end when he dies, except three things: ongoing charity, beneficial knowledge, or a righteous child who prays for him. Shared knowledge keeps giving reward even after death.

**Hadith — Sunan Ibn Majah 240**
The Prophet ﷺ said whoever teaches knowledge gets a reward like the one who acts on it, without that person's own reward being reduced at all. Teaching others adds reward — it never takes away.

**Hadith — Sahih al-Bukhari**
The Prophet ﷺ said the best among people are those who learn the Quran and teach it to others. Learning alone is not enough; teaching completes it.

**Hadith — Sahih al-Bukhari (on envy)**
The Prophet ﷺ said envy is only right in two cases: a person given wealth who spends it rightly, and a person given wisdom who acts on it and teaches it to others. Holding knowledge back out of jealousy has no place here.

## 📖 How to View These Notes

### Step 1: Install Obsidian
Download and install Obsidian from [obsidian.md](https://obsidian.md)

### Step 2: Clone this repo
```bash
git clone https://github.com/Md-Yousuf-2723/Notes_of_Btech_in_ECE.git
```

### Step 3: Open the folder in Obsidian
- Open Obsidian
- Click **"Open folder as vault"**
- Select the cloned `Notes_of_Btech_in_ECE` folder

### Step 4: Install the Excalidraw plugin
- Go to **Settings** → **Community plugins**
- Turn **OFF** "Restricted mode" (Safe mode)
- Click **Browse**
- Search for **"Excalidraw"**
- Click **Install**, then **Enable**

### Step 5: View the notes
- Open any `.md` file from the sidebar to read notes
- All my handwriiten notes will be visiable. 

✅ You're done! All notes and diagrams should now display correctly.
